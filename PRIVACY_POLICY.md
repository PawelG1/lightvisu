# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 11, 2025**

## 1. Introduction

DeckMate (LightVisu) ("we", "us", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **User Account Information**: If you create an account, we collect email address and password.
- **Preferences**: Display settings, language preferences, and customization options.
- **Communication**: If you contact us for support, we collect your messages and contact information.

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, device ID, model.
- **Usage Data**: Features accessed, duration of use, actions performed, crash reports.
- **Location Data**: We do NOT collect or use GPS location data. The App only displays navigation information based on user input.

### 2.3 Vessel Configuration Data
- The App uses vessel configuration files (JSON) stored locally on your device.
- No vessel or navigation data is transmitted to our servers.
- All calculations and visualizations are performed locally on your device.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the App functionality
- Improve and optimize the App performance
- Send technical notices and support messages
- Respond to user inquiries and support requests
- Monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends
- Prevent fraud and enhance security
- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We **DO NOT** sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share information only when:
- Required by law (subpoena, court order)
- Necessary to protect the safety or rights of users or the public
- Working with trusted service providers bound by confidentiality agreements

### 4.1 Third-Party Services
The App may use the following third-party services:
- **Google Play Services**: For app distribution and crash reporting
- **Firebase** (if enabled): For analytics and crash reporting

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them.

## 5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

### 5.1 Local Storage
- Vessel configuration and user preferences are stored locally on your device
- The App uses device encryption when available
- No sensitive data is synchronized to cloud services

## 6. Data Retention

- **Account Data**: Retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion anytime.
- **Usage Analytics**: Retained for up to 12 months for analytics purposes.
- **Device Identifiers**: Retained as needed to prevent fraud.

You can request data deletion by contacting us at the email provided below.

## 7. User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:
- **Access**: Right to know what data we collect about you
- **Correction**: Right to update or correct inaccurate data
- **Deletion**: Right to request deletion of your data ("Right to be Forgotten")
- **Portability**: Right to receive your data in a structured format
- **Objection**: Right to object to processing of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete this information immediately.

## 9. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites and services. This Privacy Policy does not apply to external sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Review their privacy policies before using their services.

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
