# PRIVACY POLICY - DeckMate

**Effective Date:** January 2025
**Last Updated:** January 2025

## 1. Introduction

DeckMate ("App", "we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

**Data Controller:** DeckMate
**Contact:** privacy@deckmate.app

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** If you create an account, we collect your name, email, and profile preferences
- **Quiz Responses:** Your answers to sailing quiz questions for educational purposes
- **Settings:** Your app preferences and display options

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Analytics:** Features used, app interaction patterns, crash logs
- **Location (Optional):** Only if you explicitly grant permission for maritime navigation features

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties
- We do NOT share or sell your data to third parties
- We do NOT integrate third-party advertising networks

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Article 6)

We process your data based on:
- **Consent (6.1.a):** When you explicitly consent to specific processing
- **Contract (6.1.b):** To provide app services you've requested
- **Legal Obligation (6.1.c):** Compliance with applicable laws
- **Legitimate Interest (6.1.f):** App improvement and user experience

## 4. How We Use Your Information

- **Service Delivery:** Provide core app functionality (3D vessel visualization, quiz)
- **App Improvement:** Analytics to understand usage patterns and improve features
- **Customer Support:** Respond to your inquiries and technical issues
- **Legal Compliance:** Meet regulatory and legal requirements
- **Security:** Detect and prevent fraud and security issues

## 5. Data Retention

- **Quiz Data:** Retained for 1 year to track learning progress (user configurable)
- **Account Data:** Retained until you delete your account, then deleted within 30 days
- **Analytics:** Anonymized data retained for 12 months
- **Crash Logs:** Retained for 90 days then automatically deleted
- **Legal Records:** Retained as required by law

## 6. Your Rights (GDPR & California Privacy Laws)

### 6.1 GDPR Rights (European Users)
- **Right to Access (Art. 15):** Request copy of your personal data
- **Right to Rectification (Art. 16):** Correct inaccurate data
- **Right to Erasure (Art. 17):** Request data deletion ("right to be forgotten")
- **Right to Restrict Processing (Art. 18):** Limit how we use your data
- **Right to Data Portability (Art. 20):** Export your data in machine-readable format
- **Right to Object (Art. 21):** Opt-out of specific processing activities
- **Right to Lodge a Complaint:** With your local data protection authority

**Data Protection Authority Contacts:**
- EU General: https://edpb.ec.europa.eu/about-edpb/board/members_en
- Poland (UODO): https://uodo.gov.pl/en/

### 6.2 California Rights (CCPA/CPRA)
- Right to know what data is collected
- Right to delete personal data
- Right to opt-out of sale/sharing of data (we do NOT sell data)
- Right to correction of inaccurate data

## 7. Data Security

- **Encryption:** All data transmitted via HTTPS/TLS encryption
- **Storage:** Device storage encrypted at OS level
- **Access Control:** Limited employee access with authentication
- **Security Updates:** Regular security patches and audits
- **Data Breaches:** Notification within 72 hours if personal data is compromised

## 9. International Data Transfers

If you are in the EU and your data is transferred to non-EU servers:
- **Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC)** are in place
- Compliance with GDPR Chapter V requirements
- Enhanced safeguards for data protection

## 10. Children's Privacy

DeckMate is intended for users 13+. We do NOT knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover such collection, we will delete it immediately. Parents with concerns should contact us at privacy@deckmate.app.

## 11. Cookies & Tracking

- **Local Storage:** App uses device local storage only (no cloud tracking)
- **No Third-Party Cookies:** We do NOT use advertising cookies
- **Analytics:** Optional, anonymized usage analytics only

## 12. California Privacy Rights (CPRA)

- **Consumer Data:** https://privacy.deckmate.app/access-data
- **Opt-Out:** In-app Settings → Privacy → Disable Analytics
- **Do Not Sell My Data:** We do NOT sell personal data
