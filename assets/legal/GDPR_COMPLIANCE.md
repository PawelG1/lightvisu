## 3. Your GDPR Rights

### Right to Access (Article 15)
- Request all personal data we hold about you
- Process: Email privacy@deckmate.app with "Data Access Request"
- Timeline: Response within 30 days
- Format: Structured, commonly-used, machine-readable format

### Right to Rectification (Article 16)
- Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- Process: In-app Settings → Account → Edit Profile
- Or email privacy@deckmate.app

### Right to Erasure (Article 17) - "Right to be Forgotten"
- Request deletion of your personal data
- **Exceptions:** Legal obligations, security, legitimate interests
- Process: In-app Settings → Account → Delete Account OR email request
- Timeline: Deletion within 30 days (some data may be retained for legal reasons)

### Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18)
- Request we stop using your data while investigating disputes
- Process: Email privacy@deckmate.app with specific request

### Right to Data Portability (Article 20)
- Export your data in portable format (JSON/CSV)
- Process: In-app Settings → Data Export OR email privacy@deckmate.app
- Timeline: Within 30 days
- Format: Structured, commonly-used, machine-readable format

### Right to Object (Article 21)
- Opt-out of:
  - Marketing communications (available in-app)
  - Analytics and profiling (Settings → Privacy)
  - Legitimate interest processing
- Process: In-app Settings → Privacy Preferences

### Rights Related to Automated Decision-Making (Article 22)
- DeckMate does NOT use automated decision-making for legal/significant effects
- All app decisions (quiz results, etc.) are informational only

## 4. Children's Data (Article 8)
